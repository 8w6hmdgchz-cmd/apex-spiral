# APEX Research Protocol v1

## Roles

- Operator: coordinates tools and safety boundaries.
- SearchSkill: finds and verifies evidence.
- Emv Entropy Skill: distills long documents into reusable skills.
- SWRs Memory: consolidates important project lessons.
- ClawG: evaluates local file/script operations in mock workspaces.
- ERA: tree-search/code-sandbox algorithm exploration.
- CoScientist: hypothesis generation, ranking, reflection, evolution.
- Robin: hypothesis → plan → experiment → analysis → mechanism.

## Evidence ledger fields

- `id`
- `source_type`
- `path_or_url`
- `claim`
- `evidence_quote`
- `confidence`
- `timestamp`
- `limitations`

## Research output types

- `protocol.md`
- `evidence-ledger.jsonl`
- `hypotheses.json`
- `experiment-plan.md`
- `analysis-report.md`
- `paper-outline.md`
- `negative-findings.md`

## Gatekeeping

- Medical/drug/target claims require strong citations and uncertainty labels.
- Experimental results require actual data or must be marked as proposed/simulated.
- No external account/API registration without explicit user action.
